Building Machine Learning Apps

Due to reaching capacity, we are no longer accepting applicants for our Summer 2023 cohort.










Course Info

Duration: 17 July 2023 - 10 August 2023, 3.5 week session

Timings: 3PM - 4:15PM (PST)

The Building Machine Learning Apps course covers topics such as data preprocessing, feature engineering, model selection, and evaluation. Students will use popular machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow and Scikit-learn to build and deploy machine learning models in real-world applications. The models covered include Random Forests, CNNs, neural networks, and more. By the end of the course, students will have practical experience building and deploying machine learning applications in a variety of settings.
(recorded classes will be made available for students who have existing time conflicts that are shared with the instructors)
