Intro to the Data Science Ecosystem

Due to reaching capacity, we are no longer accepting applicants for our Summer 2023 cohort.










Course Info

Duration: 19 June 2023 - 13 July 2023, 3.5 week session

Timings: 3PM - 4:15PM (PST)

This course provides an overview of the field of data science, including data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and modeling. Students will learn how to use the popular programming language Python to explore and analyze data sets. The course emphasizes the importance of critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and provides hands-on experience with real-world data sets. By the end of the course, students will have a basic understanding of data science and be prepared for further study or careers in the field.
(recorded classes will be made available for students who have existing time conflicts that are shared with the instructors)

Prerequisites: None

Instructor(s): Aneesha Sreerama & Shreya Kochar
